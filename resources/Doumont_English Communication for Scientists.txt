English Communication for Scientists is a brief guide on how to communicate more effectively in English, written by Jean-Luc Doumont. 

http://www.nature.com/scitable/ebooks/english-communication-for-scientists-14053993

%%
%% Comments
%%

It contains somehow the same messages that in his hand-outs, but in much more thorough and detailed. A lot of resources (slides, videos) are included. Very good if ones want to get a general review of the concepts of scientific communication, and communication in general.
